Titanfall 2

Future of War

Experience the ultimate blend of fast-paced gunplay with giant robotic powerhouses. Pilot your Titan and dominate the battlefield, or master parkour as a nimble Pilot. The balance between these two playstyles creates an unmatched competitive experience.

Campaign Story

Engage in one of the best single-player campaigns in gaming history. Join Pilot Cooper and his Titan BT-7274 in an epic journey filled with emotional moments, intense action, and unforgettable gameplay mechanics.

Multiplayer Mayhem

Fight across diverse game modes including Attrition, Capture the Flag, and Pilot vs Pilot. Team up with fellow pilots to destroy enemy Titans, complete objectives, and prove your skills on the frontier.

Wall-Running & Parkour

Titanfall 2's movement system is revolutionary. Chain wall-runs, double-jumps, and slides to perform incredible feats of acrobatics. The skill ceiling is incredibly high, rewarding players who master the mechanics.
